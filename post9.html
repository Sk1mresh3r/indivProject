Sky Bliss

Essay

How have you been? I hope the day has been treating you well, though it's ending now I hope you can look back at today with no regrets and reservations. You've earned the rest, so now it's time to relax and let yourself unwind, the day was indeed hectic and troublesome but now you are past that, congratulations!

As you take your rest do not forget to appreciate what's around you, the ground, the horizon, and the sky. These things go by unnoticed from our day to day and it really would make a world of difference if you remember to stop and appreciate sometimes. Rest up and rest well, you've earned it!
