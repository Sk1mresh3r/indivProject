Sunlit Muse

Essay

The world can be so cold sometimes, and from time to time we may crave that human connection. But, rest assured these feelings will come to pass eventualy. I know loneliness has a deeo-piercing pang but the most we can do is to cope and move along.

So dear reader, I am giving you the assurance that, there are many people in this world who feel how you feel right now. and all you would need to do is reach your hand out and take a leap into the unknown. Don't be afraid to reach out, people are nicer than one would initially think. This cold world can indeed be warm at times.
