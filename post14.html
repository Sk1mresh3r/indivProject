Twilight Dreams

Essay

Dreaming of the sky can feel blissful at times but I hope we do not forget ourselves and get lost in our dreamy imaginations. Just as we take our time to relax and decompress, we should also keep oursleves grounded lest we end up floating away into a different reality.

Remember that we are human, and we do deserve to escape from time to time but we shouldn't run away from thigns forever. As much as we do deserve to relax, we must also hold ourselves accountable for the tasks we have yet to accomplish. So there, dear reader. I hope you rest well but always remember to ground yourself, and rest true.
