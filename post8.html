As the day nears its end, let us not forget the value of meditation. Meditation isn't all that intimidating, all you need to do is find a comfortable and quiet place to sit and ensure you have a couple minutes of your time to spare.

First off, make sure you are seated in a comfortable position, next be sure to close your eyes and take deep breaths. The goal of this meditation is to focus on your breaths from the inhale to the exhale.

When your mind starts to wander, gently pull yourself back into focusing on your breaths, do this until you can no longer or enough time has passed. and congratulations! you have successfully meditated.
