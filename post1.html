Luminous Lift

Essay

As the sky begins to darken I reflect on the things that has occurred throghout the day, there may have been some confrontations, a couple moments of misunderstanding from here and there, and moments of joy and triumph. I wouldn't have this day any other way because I am at peace and my heart is calm. So, as we admire the sky and take our breaths, always remember the peace that rests from within.
