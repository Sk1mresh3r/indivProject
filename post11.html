Radiance

Poetry

Your radiance is something to be admired,
your beauty emanates and pierces every veil,
my blind eyes can feel the warmth of your aesthetic nature,
with your presence, I feel safe, I feel secure,

And as I waddle on my day-to-day,
I will greatly remember the smile you gave,
the tenderness of your precious hug,
and your company,

So as I say my closing words,
I thank you again dear friend,
for you are a friend who never leaves,
dearest, radiant, sky.
