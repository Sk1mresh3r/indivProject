About Me

Hello! My name is Jonah De Guzman;

Jonah comes from the biblical character, while De Guzman is a family name that was passed down throughout multiple generations. And as you can see, De Guzman is a fairly common name in the Philippines, and this fact can be attributed to my great great great great grandfather De Guzman. Our family has a long history in the Philippines, you would find De Guzmans in Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao.

I am currently studying Computer Science in the University of Baguio, my passion for Computer Science extends as far as the oceans go, Computers are such an interesting topic to me, from the early dawn of the creation of the calculator to the supercomputers we now know today. Computer Science has had such an interesting history and development. As fascinating as the past was, I am also keen on seeing what the future has to behold.

I created this website because I like exploring and admiring the skies. I figured that not enough people have the privelege of taking a breather and admiring the sky. So now this blog exists to show that there are things that can be missed during life's daily hustle-and-bustle. Come relax with me as we admire the skies!
