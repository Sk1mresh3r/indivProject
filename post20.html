Nimbus

Educational

Nimbus is another word associated with clouds. Adding “nimbus” means precipitation is falling from the cloud. Essentially, nimbus clouds are clouds that carry rain, snow, or hail.

There are two main types of nimbus clouds: cumulonimbus and nimbostratus. Cumulonimbus clouds are cumulus clouds that carry heavy precipitation usually in the form of rain or hail. while Nimbostratus clouds are low-hanging, dark gray clouds that block the sun completely. They make steady rain or snow, but not thunderstorms or lightning.
